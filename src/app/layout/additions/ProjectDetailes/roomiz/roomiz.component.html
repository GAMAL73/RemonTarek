overview

Roomiz allows the user to purchase furniture products through e-commerce. It provides an efficient and convenient way for users to search a wide range of furniture products, get detailed information about each product, and make purchases And deliver the product as quickly as possible.

photos

roomiz Challenges

The traditional furniture shopping experience often lacks convenience, leaving users frustrated. They struggle to track their orders and verify whether they are confirmed, placed, or shipped. Additionally, sellers frequently use stock photos instead of real product images, reducing trust. Users also face limitations in customizing products, such as changing background colors or previewing items in-store. Finding the right furniture model can be challenging, and ensuring that items fit their space is a major concern. Many users require personalized room designs, while others, accustomed to in-store shopping, hesitate to buy furniture online. Furthermore, a lack of diverse payment and shipping options creates additional friction in the purchasing process.

roomiz Solutions

To enhance the shopping experience, the platform features a user-friendly interface with smart search functionality to streamline product discovery. Real-time order tracking and seamless return options ensure a hassle-free purchase journey. The platform also displays 3D product images with a 360-degree view and allows users to customize product colors and backgrounds while offering the option to check products in-store. With smart scanning tools, users can effortlessly find their desired furniture models. Personalized room designs are available, and Augmented Reality (AR) technology enables users to visualize how furniture will fit into their space before making a purchase. The platform also supports comprehensive product research, price comparison, and quality assessment to help users make informed decisions. Additionally, multiple international payment methods, and Wallet, along with a secure checkout process, simplify transactions. Various shipping options, including store pickup and delivery tracking, further enhance convenience.
