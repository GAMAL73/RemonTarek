Project Details

ONE MORE is a fitness application designed to address the challenges athletes face by providing comprehensive workout and nutrition programs. The app offers personalized training plans, expert guidance from top trainers and doctors, and interactive exercise tutorials.

Key features include:

1- Customized Training Plans: Users can select workout programs tailored to their fitness level and goals.

2- Video Demonstrations: Each exercise includes a detailed instructional video, showcasing proper form and highlighting the targeted muscles.

3- Nutrition Guidance: Personalized meal plans to complement fitness goals and enhance performance.

4- Progress Tracking: Users can monitor their improvements over time with visual progress reports.

5- Expert Support: Direct follow-up from professional trainers and doctors for personalized advice.
